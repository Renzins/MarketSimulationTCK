================================================================================
  main_data_imputed.csv  —  COLUMN DESCRIPTIONS
================================================================================

File:     main_data_imputed.csv
Period:   2025-02-05 00:00 UTC  to  2026-05-02 23:45 UTC
Interval: 15 minutes, strictly UTC, no timezone offset

All hourly source data (EE/LV load, EE/LV wind/solar, reserves legacy,
Scipher day-ahead before Oct 2025) was expanded to 15-min by repeating the
same value four times per hour.
Turbine data was originally 10-min EET; it was imputed, converted to UTC,
and resampled to 15-min by averaging values within each bin.

NordPool intraday VWAP snapshots (at 1h and 3h before delivery) were
joined on the floored hour, so each hour's value is repeated across all
four 15-min slots.

Data was trimmed to start at 2025-02-05 to eliminate the structural gap in
reserves data that existed before that date.

================================================================================
  IMPUTATION AND CLEANING APPLIED
================================================================================

Before imputation, a rolling z-score anomaly detector (24-hour centered
window, threshold 3.5, minimum 30% nonzero neighbours) was applied to all
load, wind/solar generation, and turbine columns. Suspicious false zeros
were set to NaN and subsequently filled by linear interpolation.

Imputation strategies by column group:
  - Scipher DA forecast:        linear interpolation (limit 4 = 1 hour)
  - Scipher ID forecast:        forward-fill (limit 8 = 2 hours), then
                                fallback to scipher_da_p50_mw
  - Renewcast forecasts:        linear interpolation (limit 4)
  - MFRR SA prices:             forward-fill then back-fill (limit 4)
  - Imbalance volumes:          linear interpolation (limit 4)
  - SA-mFRR bid prices:         forward-fill then back-fill (limit 4)
  - Reserves (aFRR/FCR/mFRR):   forward-fill then back-fill (limit 4)
  - aFRR LMP:                   forward-fill (limit 4), then fill 0.0
  - aFRR bid prices:            forward-fill (limit 4), then fill 0.0
  - LT day-ahead price:         forward-fill then back-fill (limit 4)
  - Loads (actual & forecast):  linear interpolation (limit 4)
  - Wind & solar generation:    linear interpolation (limit 4);
                                solar columns clipped >= 0
  - WTG turbines:               linear interpolation (limit 4),
                                then summed into wind_park_possible
  - NordPool intraday VWAP:    forward-fill then back-fill (limit 4)
  - Imbalance price (Latvia):  joined from external files, left as NaN
                                where no matching timestamp exists

Columns removed after imputation:
  - All MFRR Direct Activation (DA) columns (mfrr_da_*): too sparse to be
    useful (Latvia 94% NaN, Lithuania 20% NaN).
  - All individual turbine columns (wtg*_possible_power): replaced by the
    aggregated wind_park_possible column.

datetime_utc
    Timestamp marking the START of each 15-minute period, in UTC.
    Format: YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS  (e.g. 2025-03-15 14:30:00 = 14:30–14:45 UTC)

================================================================================
  SECTION 1 — WIND FARM GENERATION FORECASTS  (Vanessa / Targale, Latvia)
================================================================================

Source: Scipher.Fx forecast accuracy reports for the "Vanessa" wind farm
        (located at Targale, Latvia; installed capacity 58.8 MW).
        Only the P50 (median) forecast is retained. The day-ahead file
        transitions from hourly resolution to 15-min on 2025-09-26; the
        intraday file is 15-min throughout. For hourly slots the same
        P50 value is repeated at :00, :15, :30 and :45.

 1  scipher_da_p50_mw
        Scipher day-ahead (R1 revision) P50 forecast of active power output
        for the Vanessa wind farm, in MW.
        Imputation: linear interpolation (limit 1 hour).
        Range: 0.01 – 58.80 MW

 2  scipher_id_p50_mw
        Scipher intraday (latest revision available) P50 forecast of active
        power output for the Vanessa wind farm, in MW.
        Imputation: forward-fill (limit 2 hours), then fallback to
        scipher_da_p50_mw for any remaining gaps.
        Range: 0.00 – 58.80 MW

Source: Renewcast forecast for Targale / Vanessa.

 3  renewcast_continuous_capped_kw
        Renewcast latest intraday (continuous) forecast of total farm output,
        in kW, capped at installed capacity (58,800 kW = 58.8 MW).
        Imputation: linear interpolation (limit 1 hour).
        Range: 0 – 58,800 kW

 4  renewcast_dayahead_capped_kw
        Renewcast day-ahead forecast of total farm output, in kW,
        capped at installed capacity (58,800 kW).
        Imputation: linear interpolation (limit 1 hour).
        Range: 0 – 58,800 kW

================================================================================
  SECTION 10 — WIND FARM POSSIBLE POWER  (Vanessa / Targale, Latvia)
================================================================================

Source: Targale_turbine_possible_power_EET.xlsx
        Aggregated from 14 individual turbine "Grid Production Possible Power
        Average" readings from the SCADA system of the Targale (Vanessa) wind
        farm in Latvia.

Processing applied:
  1. Missing turbine values at any timestamp were imputed with the mean of
     the remaining turbines that had valid readings at that same timestamp,
     BEFORE any resampling.
  2. Timestamps were originally in EET (UTC+2 fixed offset) and converted
     to UTC by subtracting 2 hours.
  3. Original 10-min data was resampled to 15-min by averaging values
     within each bin.
  4. Individual turbines linearly interpolated (limit 1 hour) and underwent
     rolling z-score cleaning for false zeros.
  5. All 14 turbines summed into a single column; individual turbine columns
     removed.

Units: kW (sum of 14 turbines). Maximum rated: 58,800 kW (14 × 4,200 kW).

86  wind_park_possible
        Total wind farm possible power output (kW), sum of turbines WTG01,
        WTG02, WTG03, WTG13–WTG18, WTG21–WTG25.

================================================================================
  SECTION 11 — NORDPOOL INTRADAY STATISTICS  (Latvia bidding zone)
================================================================================

Source: NordPool Data API, Intraday/HourlyStatistics/Yearly/ByArea endpoint
        for area LV. Hourly resolution; each hour's value is repeated across
        all four 15-min slots within that hour.
        These are NOT rolling averages; each value is a fixed snapshot of the
        volume-weighted average price (VWAP) of intraday trades for that
        delivery hour, captured at a specific lead time before delivery.
        Linear interpolation was applied during the fetch phase to fill
        sporadic gaps in the hourly source. Any remaining NaN after merge
        was forward-filled then back-filled (limit 1 hour).
        Units: EUR/MWh.

87  averagePriceLast1H
        Volume-weighted average intraday market price for the LV delivery
        hour, observed 1 hour before the time of delivery (EUR/MWh).

88  averagePriceLast3H
        Volume-weighted average intraday market price for the LV delivery
        hour, observed 3 hours before the time of delivery (EUR/MWh).

================================================================================
  SECTION 12 — FINAL IMBALANCE PRICE  (Latvia)
================================================================================

Source: Baltic Balancing Market imbalance-prices files (two files spanning
        2025-01-01 to 2027-01-01). Semicolon-delimited with European decimal
        format (comma as decimal separator). The "Final" column is used;
        "Preliminary" is discarded.
        Data is filtered to area = Latvia and joined on datetime_from matching
        datetime_utc. Both source files are concatenated and de-duplicated;
        only the date range present in the main CSV is retained.
        Units: EUR/MWh.

89  final_imbalance_price_latvia
        Final imbalance settlement price for the Latvia bidding zone
        (EUR/MWh). This is the ex-post price applied to imbalance energy
        in each 15-minute settlement period.

================================================================================
  NOTES ON DATA QUALITY
================================================================================

- Data starts at 2025-02-05 to avoid the structural reserves gap.
- MFRR Direct Activation (DA) columns removed due to extreme sparsity.
- Individual turbine columns replaced by aggregated wind_park_possible.
- Rolling z-score anomaly detection (24h window, z > 3.5) was applied to
  loads, wind/solar, and turbine columns to replace false zeros with
  interpolated values before imputation.
- aFRR LMP and aFRR bid columns: NaN after short forward-fill was set to
  0.0, reflecting periods with no activation or no submitted bids.
- Some columns may still contain residual NaN if gaps exceeded the 1-hour
  imputation limit; check the post-imputation report from the script.
